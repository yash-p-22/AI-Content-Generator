## Packages
dexie | IndexedDB wrapper for local persistence of generation history
dexie-react-hooks | React hooks for Dexie
framer-motion | Smooth animations and page transitions

## Notes
- Dexie is used for local storage of generation history and client-side rate limiting (100/24h).
- The reference image is imported statically from @assets.
- Wouter is used for routing.
- The UI uses a deep indigo and electric violet palette with a subtle grid background.
